## Key Features

### Token Deployment
- **Multiple Launch Modes**: Choose between Standard (simple) or Bundled (advanced) launches
- **Quick Launch Configs**: Save and reuse your deployment settings
- **Twitter Integration**: Deploy tokens directly from tweets with one click
- **Image Tools**: Upload, crop, search Google Images, or generate ASCII art
- **Vamp Token**: Copy metadata from existing tokens instantly

### Volume & Trading
- **Volume Bot**: Automate trading with randomized, natural-looking patterns
- **Sniper Wallets**: Configure multiple wallets to buy at launch
- **Insta-Sell**: Create organic volume with automatic selling
- **Multi-Launch**: Launch the same token multiple times for maximum effect
- **Activity Generation**: Make wallets look established with simulated trading

### Wallet Management
- **Wallet Groups**: Organize up to 20 wallets per group
- **Distribution Tools**: Send SOL to all wallets with one click
- **Mixer Integration**: Break bubblemap connections for privacy (2% fee)
- **Master Wallets**: Switch between multiple master wallets
- **Individual Control**: Manage each wallet separately when needed

### Rewards & Earnings
- **Cashback System**: Earn up to 10% cashback on trading fees based on volume
- **3-Tier Referrals**: Earn 30% (direct), 3% (indirect), and 2% (extended) commissions
- **Unlimited Earnings**: No cap on referral commissions
- **Instant Claims**: Withdraw rewards to your master wallet anytime
